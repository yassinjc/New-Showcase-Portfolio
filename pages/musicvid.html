A MUSIC VIDEO EDITED TO ENHANCE MOOD AND RHYTHM

TRANSLATING MUSIC INTO VISUALS

Alongside my IT studies, music plays a major role in my creative work.

For my Sprint X project in semester 2, I combined this passion with my studies by creating a full music video for one of my own songs.

The editing process was done in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. The final video focuses on fast-paced editing, rhythmic cuts, and visual effects to match the energy and flow of the music.



Together with friends, we filmed the video using two different cameras: a Nikon D780 and an iPhone.

The iPhone footage was recorded with the Rarevision VHS app, adding a raw, nostalgic visual layer to the project.
