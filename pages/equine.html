A DASHBOARD TO MONITOR HORSES AND THEIR WELLBEING

FROM EQUINE DATA TO MEANINGFUL INSIGHT

During this project, we designed a dashboard that supports the monitoring of equine welfare by translating complex data into clear, interpretable signals.

The interface scales from single-horse views to multi-horse overviews, enabling users to quickly identify horses that need attention while maintaining domain-level welfare visibility.
