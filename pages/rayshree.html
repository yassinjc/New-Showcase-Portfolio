A WEBSITE FOR THE HAIR & MAKE-UP ARTIST RAYSHREE

TRANSLATING BEAUTY INTO A DIGITAL EXPERIENCE

For my semester 3 passion project, I created a website for the hair & make-up artist Rayshree, designed to clearly showcase her services and work.

After user testing, the design was developed into a fully functional website. The final result is a platform that presents Rayshree's work and services in a clear and professional way.

After research and several conversations with Rayshree, a clear foundation for the website was defined.

The design evolved through multiple iterations, shaped by feedback and collaboration.
