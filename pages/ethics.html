AN OVERVIEW PLATFORM FOR ETHICAL ISSUES IN ICT

ETHICS, MADE ACCESSIBLE

During my internship at Fontys ICT, I developed an interactive tool for the Research Group for Interaction Design, focused on ethics in ICT research.

An interactive “Test Yourself” quiz encourages active learning. Combining direct feedback with a clean, accessible interface and in-page document previews that support ethical reflection.

The platform introduces six ethical principles, with Autonomy explored through definitions, examples, FAQs, and supporting materials.
