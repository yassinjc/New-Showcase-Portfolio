A WEBSITE FOR THE MUSIC ARTIST AR-JANE

BRINGING THE ARTIST'S SOUND TO SCREEN

This project started with capturing a feeling rather than defining a style. Ar-Jane's sound, atmosphere, and presence formed the basis for every visual choice that followed.

The final result is a flexible website that moves with the artist. A space where music, visuals, and feeling come together in a way that can grow over time.



Through sketches, brand elements, and digital concepts, the website slowly took shape.

Each iteration brought the design closer to her identity, guided by constant feedback and refinement.
